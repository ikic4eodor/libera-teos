About Us

Welcome To Libera De Lux Libera De Lux is a company from Pirot, a small town in eastern Serbia. We are specialized in trading frozen, dry and fresh fruits, vegetables and forest fruits. With huge experience in this niche our team of managers is operating on the territory of Serbia, Bosnia and Hercegovina, Makedonia and Montenegro. With quality and stability of our services, we have proven as a reliable supplier of big companies in EU, Turkey and the Russian federation.
